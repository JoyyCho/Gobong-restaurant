About

Welcome to Gobongbap, a charming eatery where the vibrant flavors of Korean street food and cupbab come to life! For a quick and satisfying meal, head over to our cupbab station, where delectable rice and various toppings are served in convenient cups, ensuring a delicious and fuss-free dining experience.

At our bunsik corner, savor the chewy and spicy delight of spicy rice cake and rappok. Also, there is deep fried dumplings with the choice of pork, beef, and vegetables. Plus, you can add on eff, dumplings, gimmale, cheddar cheese on top of our bunsik.

Immerse yourself in the art of gimbap, where skilled chefs craft these delightful rolls with precision and care. Each bite unveils a symphony of textures and tastes, from the tender rice to the fresh vegetables and savory proteins. Our gimbap is a true celebration of Korean culinary craftsmanship.

Gobongbap is not just a restaurant; it's a journey into the heart of Korean street food culture. Whether you're craving the nostalgic flavors of bunsik, the convenience of cupbab, or the exquisite taste of gimbap, our restaurant promises an unforgettable gastronomic adventure. Come, experience the soulful delights of Korean cuisine in every bite!
